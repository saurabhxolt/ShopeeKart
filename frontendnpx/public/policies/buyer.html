1. Strict No-Cancellation Policy

Because our sellers start preparing your order immediately to ensure fast delivery, cancellations are NOT allowed.

  • Once an order is placed and payment is successful, the order is locked. You cannot cancel or modify it. Please double-check your cart, size, and address before paying.

2. Strict Return & Refund Policy

We host small businesses and ethnic wear sellers. To protect them from fraud and high logistics costs, we have a very strict return policy.

  • ❌ What CANNOT be returned: We DO NOT accept returns for reasons of personal preference. This includes:
    • "I changed my mind."
    • "I don't like the color." (Slight color variations happen due to phone screen settings).
    • "The size doesn't fit me." (Please read the size charts carefully before buying).
    • "The fabric doesn't feel like what I imagined."
  • ✅ What CAN be returned: You can only return an item if:
    • The item is physically torn, broken, or damaged inside the package.
    • The Seller sent you the completely wrong item (e.g., you ordered a blue saree, you received a red shirt).

3. The Mandatory Unboxing Video Rule (CRITICAL)

If you receive a damaged or wrong item, you must prove it. We will automatically reject any return request that does not have a valid unboxing video.

How to record the video:

  1. Do not open the package before recording.
  2. Start recording and show the sealed package from all sides, including a clear shot of the shipping label with your name and address.
  3. Keep the camera rolling (no cuts, pauses, or edits) while you cut open the package and take the item out.
  4. Clearly show the damage or the wrong item to the camera.

4. How to Claim Your Refund

  • You must email support@arivkart.com with your Order ID and the Unboxing Video within 48 hours of receiving the package.
  • Once the Seller verifies the video, a return pickup will be arranged.
  • You will get a full refund to your original payment method 5-7 business days after the Seller receives the damaged item back in their warehouse.

  3. तुम्ही पॅकेज कापून उघडताना आणि वस्तू बाहेर काढताना कॅमेरा चालू ठेवा (कोणतेही कट, पॉज किंवा एडिटिंग करू नका).
  4. कॅमेरामध्ये झालेले नुकसान किंवा चुकीची वस्तू स्पष्टपणे दाखवा.
