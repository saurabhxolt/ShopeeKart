(This Privacy Policy describes how ArivKart collects, uses, and shares your personal information.)

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to all our users. This includes:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and shipping address provided during registration or checkout.
  • Seller Information: Business name, GSTIN, PAN, and bank account details for processing payouts.
  • Technical Data: IP address, device type, and browser details collected via cookies to improve website performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to process orders, verify accounts, and handle customer support. We also use traffic logs to analyze how users interact with ArivKart to improve the shopping experience.

3. Sharing of Data

  • With Sellers: We share Buyer names and addresses with Sellers only to fulfill and ship your orders.
  • Logistics Partners: We share contact details with delivery partners to ensure your products reach you.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose information if required by law or to prevent fraud.

4. Payment Security

ArivKart **does not store** your full credit card or UPI details. All payments are processed through secure, PCI-compliant third-party gateways (like Razorpay or Cashfree).

5. Data Protection & Your Rights

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. You have the right to access, update, or request the deletion of your personal information by contacting our support team.
